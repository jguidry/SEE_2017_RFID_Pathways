PROFESSIONAL PATHWAYS

Choose a pathway! As you journey throughout Expedition, you will identify as one of the roles below. The pathway you select will determine what you see at each exhibit whenever you scan your card, so choose wisely!

Engineer

Engineers focus on designing tools for the experiments. As an engineer you will learn how professional engineers construct and apply these tools in their careers.

Recommended for: Designers, Problem Solvers

Biologist

Biologists study living organism and their influences on their enviroment. As a biologist you will learn how professional biologists use scientific method to learn about how living organisms function.

Recommended for: Scientists, Animal Lovers

Climatologist

Climatologists hate people like Josh because he thinks they don't matter. As a climatologist, you will learn how to care for the planet unlike Josh.

Recommended for: Enviromentalists, Polar Bear Lovers
